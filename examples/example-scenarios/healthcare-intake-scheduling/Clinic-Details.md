# Willow Creek Health Clinic (Demo)

## Clinic Overview

- Clinic name: Willow Creek Health Clinic
- Care model: Primary care with same-week urgent visits
- Patient age range: Adults and children (age 5+)
- Languages supported: English and Spanish

- Billing phone: +1 (415) 555-0191
- Email: support@willowcreek-demo.com
- Address: 245 Harbor Lane, Suite 200, San Francisco, CA 94107

## Hours

- Monday-Friday: 8:00 AM-6:00 PM
- Saturday: 9:00 AM-1:00 PM
- Sunday: Closed

## Appointment Types

- New patient visit (45 minutes)
- Follow-up visit (20 minutes)
- Annual wellness exam (40 minutes)
- Pediatric sick visit (20 minutes)
- Behavioral health consultation (50 minutes)

## Visit Formats

- In-person appointments: Available for all visit types
- Virtual appointments: Available for follow-up and select urgent concerns
- Preferred telehealth platform: Secure browser-based video link

## Insurance and Payment

- Accepted: Aetna, Blue Cross, Cigna, UnitedHealthcare, Medicare, most PPO plans
- Self-pay new patient visit: $165
- Self-pay follow-up visit: $95
- Copays are due at check-in

## Intake and Preparation

- New patients should complete intake forms at least 24 hours before their first visit
- Please bring a photo ID, insurance card, and medication list
- For telehealth visits, join 5 minutes early and test camera/microphone

## Parking and Transportation

- On-site parking garage: Available in the same building (entrance on Harbor Lane)
- Parking validation: First 90 minutes validated for clinic visits
- Street parking: Limited metered parking nearby
- Public transit: The clinic is a 6-minute walk from the Harbor Station stop
- Accessibility: Elevator access and ADA parking spots are available

## Rescheduling and Cancellation

- Cancellation window: At least 24 hours before appointment time
- Late cancellation fee: $35
- No-show fee: $50

## Late Arrival Policy

- 0-10 minutes late: Usually still seen
- 11-15 minutes late: Provider discretion; visit may be shortened
- More than 15 minutes late: May require rescheduling

## Prescriptions and Refills

- Refill requests: Submit through patient portal or call main line
- Standard refill turnaround: 1-2 business days
- Controlled substances: Requires provider review and may require follow-up visit

## Lab Work and Results

- Routine lab turnaround: 2-4 business days
- Specialty lab turnaround: 5-10 business days
- Critical results: Communicated by phone
- Normal results: Shared through patient portal

## Urgent vs Emergency Guidance

- For urgent but non-life-threatening concerns, call the clinic first
- For severe chest pain, breathing difficulty, stroke symptoms, or major trauma, call 911 immediately

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do you accept walk-ins?
We reserve a limited number of same-day urgent slots. Calling ahead is recommended.

### Can I switch an in-person visit to virtual?
In many cases yes, depending on your symptoms and provider availability.

### How quickly can I get a new patient appointment?
Typical wait time is 3-7 business days.
